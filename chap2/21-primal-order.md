### FAIRE-seq<a name="e"></a>
FAIRE-seq [[6]](http://www.genome.org/cgi/doi/10.1101/gr.5533506) is **Formaldehyde-assisted isolation of regulatory elements followed by sequencing**. Nucleosome-bound DNA is crosslinked and removed by phenol–chloroform extraction and the remaining nucleosome- free DNA is analyzed by microarray or sequencing.
